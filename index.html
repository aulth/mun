About the event

Jamia Millia Islamia will hold its first ever Model United Nations conference as a part of Tangelo, Jamia's first and biggest techno-cultural fest. Adapting to the changing trends in the college scene around the nation, Jamia Engineering Etudiants Association has taken the initiative to host this MUN conference.

Being the first, the Secretariat will try its level best to ensure that we set up a whole new standard in the collegiate MUN circuit.
